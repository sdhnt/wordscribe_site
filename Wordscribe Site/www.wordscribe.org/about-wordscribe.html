How does wordscribe work?

Step 1:

Select a Worscribe project to work on


Step 2:

Send us your application through the button on the APPLY page.


Step 3:

We'll give you access to collaborative writing resources and sample content.

You only write a few chapters - much easier than writing a whole 200,000-word book!


Step 4:

Submit your work!


Step 5:

We'll proof-read your work, compile text from other authors like you, make covers, register and ISBN and publish your co-authored book on amazon for sale!


Step 6:

You are now a published author and you can earn royalty for your work!


Step 7:

If the book sells over 500 copies online then we'll approach a paperback publisher to publish your work!
